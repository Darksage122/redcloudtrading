Product & Services

REDCLOUD TRADE LLC was started in New York City in the year 2013 with a vision of making Trading across geographic boundaries as simple as possible. Adil Mirza, Founder and CEO, has been involved in Importing and Exporting for the last 5 years. We currently have a strong presence in USA, India and Australia and are expanding our reach globally. We are planning to establish a presence with South American International companies. We are consistently striving to offer the best solutions to problems with international trade that companies face on a daily basis. RedCloud currently specializes in import and export markets in India, USA and Australia. We plan to expand our global presence through collaboration with global partners and knowledge of various global commodity markets.
